Do you have a bunch of stuff you’d like to get rid of? Then book a booth at the university flea market on May 20, 2026, between 11:00 a.m. and 4:00 p.m. The flea market will take place in the university lobby as part of Sustainability Days, and you can start setting up as early as 10:30 a.m. If you have any questions, please contact [greenoffice@uni-konstanz.de](mailto:greenoffice@uni-konstanz.de).
