Do you enjoy writing but haven’t quite found the courage yet? Are you bursting with ideas but don’t know how to turn them into a piece of writing? Do you enjoy writing but are running out of ideas?  
Then come to our workshop and meet the Wasserzeichen editorial team. We’ll talk about writing experiences, give you tips for short stories, poems, or literary essays, and write as much as we can.  
Interested? Please sign up by email at [wasserzeichen.hsg@uni-konstanz.de](mailto:wasserzeichen.hsg@uni-konstanz.de) (limited number of participants).
