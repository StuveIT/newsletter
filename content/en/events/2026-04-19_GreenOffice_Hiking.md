As part of our theme month "Health", we invite you to a joint hiking tour through the Marienschlucht and along the northeastern side of the Bodanrück. You can expect forest, rocks and the lake as well as a ruin and nice people. Pack enough to drink and a packed lunch, because we will be on the road for about five hours with breaks. We look forward to seeing you! You can register [here](https://forms.gle/FcsQr4s6JwMQwuTU9)  
Start: By public transport at 08:40 a.m. at Konstanz train station or by bike from 8:10 a.m. from the bicycle bridge (Herosé side). The meeting point is the station Langenrein, Bürgerhaus around 09:20 a.m.
