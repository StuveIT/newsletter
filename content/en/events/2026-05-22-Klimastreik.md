---
title: Climate strike in front of City Hall
date: 22th May
who: Fridays for Future and Students for Future
where: in front of the Konstanz City Hall
time: "11:00-12:00"
---

For several weeks now, students in Konstanz have been taking to the streets again to protest for the climate. Right in front of City Hall, they are calling for a faster socio-ecological transformation of the city of Konstanz. On May 22, we as students want to join them and show that we stand behind them and that there are many of us.
