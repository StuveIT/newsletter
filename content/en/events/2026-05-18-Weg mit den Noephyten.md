Do you feel the need to literally “get your hands in the dirt” again? Then come join our invasive plant removal event in partnership with BUND. Invasive plants are species that do not belong to a habitat’s natural vegetation and have been spread worldwide by humans. This includes the impatiens, which has taken hold in the reed beds surrounding the canal landscape at our university. Together with BUND, we want to tackle this and other landscape conservation tasks that promote biodiversity at our university. Come by and lend a hand!!
