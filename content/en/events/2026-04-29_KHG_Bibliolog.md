Bibliolog invites you to discover biblical texts in a lively and personal way. Together we enter into a selected biblical story and put ourselves in the shoes of the characters appearing in it. Through questions and short impulses, space is created to fill the "interstitial spaces" of the narrative with one's own thoughts, experiences and perspectives. In this way, the Bible becomes a place of dialogue between the old text and our lives today.The evening will be organized by Thurid Brümmel, Department of Bibliolog at the Institute for Pastoral Education.
