---
title: Two plays, one evening
who: Unitheater Konstanz
where: Apollo Kulturhaus (Konstanzerstr. 23, CH-8280 Kreuzlingen)
---

Unitheater Konstanz is staging the plays “Liebe Lotte” by Serafina Joy Bach and Lucie Victoria Charlotte Wieske and “kein morgen mehr” by Hannah Grauer at the Apollo Kulturhaus in Kreuzlingen. The plays will be performed on February 2, 3, and 5 at 8:00 p.m. and on February 8 at 2:00 p.m. and 6:00 p.m. Doors open 30 minutes before each performance. The plays are in German.
