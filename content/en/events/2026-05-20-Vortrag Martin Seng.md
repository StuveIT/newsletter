"Der deutsche Film unter dem Hakenkreuz – Das lange Leben der NS-Propaganda" deals with the systematic misuse of film after 1933 for Nazi propaganda and how anti-Semitic productions and so-called perseverance films influenced society. The films "Triumph of the Will" and "Olympia" by director Leni Riefenstahl are of particular importance, whose visual language is still shaped by other media (including music videos and video games). The focus is on the denazification of Nazi film and how the propagandists had a great career even after 1945. Overall, the lecture also shows how the Nazi visual language is increasingly showing up again on social media today and how influential the images from back then still are.
