---
title: Valentine's Special
date: Sat, February 14, 2026
who: University of Konstanz Traveller Network
where: Lucerne, Mount Rigi, and St. Gallen
time: 7:00 a.m. to 9:00 p.m.
---

The University of Konstanz Traveller Network is organizing a trip to Lucerne, Mount Rigi, and St. Gallen on Valentine's Day. The bus will depart from Konstanz at 7:00 a.m. and head first to Lucerne. This will be followed by a guided hike to Rigi Klum. In the evening, participants can enjoy the Große Wiler Monsterkonzert concert before returning to Konstanz at around 9:00 p.m. Students under the age of 25 pay a fee of €25, while students over the age of 25 pay €30. More information is available on Instagram.
