title: "Lake Heat and Solar Expansion: Experiencing the Energy Transition on Campus"
date: 5th May
who: Sustainability and Green Office
where: Universitätsstraße 10, Meeting Point i-Punkt
time: "12:00-13:00"
---

The tour at the University of Konstanz offers insights into two key future projects for sustainable energy supply on campus. The focus is on the new energy center (currently under construction), where, starting in late 2027, large heat pumps using water from Lake Constance will provide a significant portion of the campus’s heating energy. Participants will learn on-site how the use of lake heat works and how it contributes to reducing CO₂ emissions. In addition, the tour offers a view of the university’s roofs, which are equipped with PV modules. There, visitors can see how the university systematically uses its space to generate electricity, taking an important step toward a climate-neutral energy supply.
