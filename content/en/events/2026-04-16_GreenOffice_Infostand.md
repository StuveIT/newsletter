Since May 2024, the association "Radeln ohne Alter Konstanz e.V."  on the streets of Constance to give seniors and people with mobility disabilities their "right to wind in their hair". With orange rickshaws, they take people out into the fresh air free of charge, to Seestraße, to the cemetery, to the Loretto Forest or to places in the city that they want to visit, because they may associate memories with it. The association is always happy to welcome new volunteer pilots! Interested parties can contact [info@radelnohnealter-kn.de](mailto:info@radelnohnealter-kn.de) or simply drop by the information booth in front of the campus café on April 16.
