This anti-littering campaign aims to raise awareness about the careless disposal of cigarette butts, which allows toxins to seep into our groundwater and soil (<https://www.bund-bremen.net/meer/stoppt-kippen-in-der-umwelt/>). Together, we’ll be handing out posters and pocket ashtrays on campus. Join us!
