Uncomfortable Topics in Earthy Tones: All the paintings are created according to an essayistic principle, in which a thought is articulated within a painting. The themes span philosophy, literature, and science. In his large-format paintings, Sandro Fedelini seeks to raise socially critical questions using acrylic and oil.
