The Markustheater takes you back to the origins of Western Christian culture: the entire Gospel of Mark in 90 minutes—performed by 15 actors right in the midst of the audience. Experience the fascinating story of Jesus Christ up close: storms, paralytics, tax collectors, the confrontation with the religious establishment, and the victory over death. A unique theatrical evening that brings the ancient text to life.
