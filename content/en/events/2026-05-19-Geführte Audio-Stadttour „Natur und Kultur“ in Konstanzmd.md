With the “Nature and Culture” audio guide, BUND Konstanz and the Lake Constance Nature Museum invite you to take a walk. You’ll discover spots in downtown Constance where nature and culture blend in a special way. After about two hours and 18 stops, the tour ends at the Lake Constance Nature Museum in Sealife.  This isn’t just for newcomers—longtime residents of Constance can also get to know their city from a new perspective.  
Internationally friendly. Unfortunately, the “Nature and Culture” audio guide through the city of Constance is only available in German, but your guide will provide a short summary in English at each stop.
