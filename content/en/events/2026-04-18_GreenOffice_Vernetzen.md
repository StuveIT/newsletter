With waffles and coffee we want to network, get to know each other and spend a nice afternoon together. All sustainable university groups and Konstanz initiatives that are active in this field are invited, as well as committed or interested individuals. Most of the time is reserved for casual exchange and the plastering of the waffles, but there will also be one or two program items. Information will follow by email. Feel free to register at [greenoffice@uni-konstanz.de](mailto:greenoffice@uni-konstanz.de) . This will be followed by the third meeting of Konstanz's sustainable initiatives to plan Public Sustainability Week 2026 from 6:00 p.m. to 7:00 p.m. All sustainable initiatives are cordially invited to participate!
