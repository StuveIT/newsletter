For the first time, in collaboration with the Zeppelin Museum in Friedrichshafen, we are offering an escape room event for groups of 5 to 6 people, including a guided tour (60-minute tour + 45-minute escape room). You can register as a group or individually [on our website](https://www.stuve-uni-kn.de/2026/03/19/zeppelinmuseum/) by April 8; the cost is €8 per person.
The event will be conducted in German.
