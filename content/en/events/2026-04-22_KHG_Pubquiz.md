We cordially invite you to a joint service and subsequent quiz evening in the Catholic University Community! Whether questions of faith, worldly knowledge or curious facts - there is something for everyone here. In small teams, you compete in brotherly competition: with humour, heart and togetherness.

- Drinks and snacks are available.
- Bring your friends with you - or find new ones locally.
- No previous knowledge necessary - just the joy of guessing! 

  Registration is not required - just come and play!
