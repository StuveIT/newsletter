Endo…what? Endometriosis is a chronic condition that is often accompanied by severe pain and limitations in daily life and affects approximately 10–15% of women\*. Yet many people have never heard of it, and those affected constantly have to explain what it is. Would you like to know what endometriosis is, what the condition entails, or whether you might be affected yourself? Then come to the lecture hosted by the Lake Constance Endometriosis Support Group, featuring medical input from the Endometriosis Center in Constance.
