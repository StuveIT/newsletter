---
title: Between Privilege and Liberation — A Workshop on Patriarchy and Feminism for Everyone
date: 9th March
who: Green Office Uni Konstanz
where: Cafe Mondial
time: "18:30 to 20:30"
---

Patriarchy? What is that again? And why should I (even as a man) work to dismantle it? Together, we want to approach these questions in a system-critical, materialistic fundamentals workshop. We will discuss the effects, background, and strategies for overcoming patriarchal rule. We will start by getting to know each other and discussing your questions about equality. Using various interactive methods, we will then look at topics such as gender, care work, love, and power together. Open, realistic, and in exchange.
