The lecture period is coming to an end and the exam phase is in full swing everywhere. We wish you every success in your exams!

We know that this can be a very stressful time. That's why we would like to draw your attention to the services offered by Nightline and the university. Nightline is a free and anonymous listening service provided by students for students and is available on Wednesdays, Fridays, Saturdays, and Sundays between 8 p.m. and midnight on 07531 20 68 86. The Central Student Advisory Service offers a wide range of counseling and workshops for students. You can also contact the Seezeit Psychotherapeutic Counseling Center at any time. You are not alone!

## What happened at StuVe last month

- StuPa and FSK have voted: David Gerhardt and Robert Keller are now chair of StuVe. will be supporting the previous chairs, Dominik Shrestha and Robin Weyers.
- Members have also been elected to the student election committee. With four of its six members now in place, the committee is ready to begin planning the student elections to be held in June.
- The AStA has approved €15,000 for the continuation of free legal advice in 2026. It has also approved approximately €30,000 for the continuation of the culture ticket.
- The Senate has decided to change the lecture slots starting in the summer semester of 2027, so that there will be a 30-minute break between classes instead of 15 minutes. This decision was made against the position of the student council conference and the student senators. More information can be found here.
- The StuVe has a new website: All information and news can now be found at stuve-uni-kn.de!

## Who are we?

We are the student representatives at the University of Konstanz (StuVe) and represent the interests of students, as well as organizing and promoting projects. We are here for you if you have any problems or concerns, or if you would like to get involved—feel free to write to us!
